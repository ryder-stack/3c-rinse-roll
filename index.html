Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is touch-free really safer than a brush wash?

Yes. Our system uses high-pressure water and specialized foams — no brushes, no cloth strips, nothing that physically touches your paint. That means no micro-scratches, swirl marks, or snagged trim.

Can I bring a truck, van, or lifted vehicle?

Absolutely. Our bay accommodates most pickups, full-size SUVs, and light-duty vans. If you're over 7'6" tall or towing, give us a call first and we'll let you know.

How does the Unlimited Club work?

Pick a plan, sign up at the wash, and your license plate becomes your pass. Pull up any time and the gate opens. Cancel online whenever — no contract, no cancellation fee.

Do you accept cash?

Cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay work at all bays. Cash is accepted at the self-serve bay and via the change machine.

Are you really open at 3am?

Yep. The lights stay on, the bays stay open, every single day — holidays included.
